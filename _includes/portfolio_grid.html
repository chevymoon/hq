Where big crypto and the people meet.



Nika is a token for contract development and custom interactions with the Nika blockchain. Amongst many things we plan on implementing staking, oracles, legal documentation stored on the ethereum chain and personal IDs for custom databases. We'll also use our own brand of Nika tokens to create an off-chain mobile cryptocurrency network. Our tokens will be distributed in a huge airdrop and implement deflationary tokenomics as well as burn-free transactions. We estimate it will take five years for launch as such, there are implemented burn-rates built in for five years.

By sending Nika to various contracts we own, you'll be able to get various information, do lending, upload legal documents, receive approval, get a new Nika credit rating and do many other things in a decentralized fashion. We are currently building our data storage contract which will allow users to have a tiny database at the cost of some gas and one Nika. Contract development will occur over the next six months and will be entirely decentralized. We'll use the funds of selling Nika tokens on exchanges to fund development.

We aim on using huge numbers, large funds and superior leadership to protect the interests of the cryptocurrency sphere. This project aims to become the biggest airdrop token of all time. Afterwards, we will work on securing venture capital funding, political legitimacy and work towards the concerns our userbase has. We will use our airdrop to distribute fairly 5 billion tokens while keeping an additional 5 billion (50%) for dev funding. We have implemented transaction caps to prevent whales hoarding too much too quickly.

We have also implemented a burn rate and a cap against any exchange owning more than 10% of the supply, to prevent downtrends. The burn rate goes down to 0% after the course of 5 years to encourage HODLing until we have reached altitude. We don't have a whitepaper or professional site, we'll focus on only what we need at the moment. We don't necessarily aim to focus on one area, but instead use our massive economies of scale to succeed. In short, we aim to be the Amazon of the cryptocurrency sphere.
